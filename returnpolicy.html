Return Policy

At SHOP I.O, we strive to ensure your satisfaction with every purchase. If for any reason you are not completely satisfied with your order, we are here to assist you. Please review our return policy below to understand how returns are processed:

  1. Eligibility for Returns
    • Returns are accepted within 30 days from the date of delivery.
    • To be eligible for a return, items must be unused, in their original condition, and in the original packaging.
  2. Initiation of Returns:
    • To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team at shopioreturndept@gmail.com with your order number and the reason for the return.
    • Our customer support team will guide you through the return process and provide you with the necessary instructions.
  3. Return Shipping:
    • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to an error on our part (e.g., wrong item shipped or item arrived damaged).
    • We recommend using a trackable shipping method to ensure that the return is received.
  4. Refund Process:
    • Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item.
    • If the return is approved, a refund will be processed to your original method of payment. Please note that shipping costs are non-refundable.
  5. Exchanges:
    • If you wish to exchange an item, please follow the return process outlined above and place a new order for the desired item.
  6. Damaged or Defective Items:
    • In the rare event that you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer support team immediately. We will work to resolve the issue and may request photos or additional information to facilitate the process.
  7. Final Sale Items:
    • Certain items may be marked as "final sale" and are not eligible for return. Please check the product description before making your purchase.
  8. Contact Information:
    • For any questions or concerns regarding returns, please contact our customer support team at shopioreturndept@gmail.com.

Note:

  • SHOP I.O reserves the right to refuse returns that do not meet the above criteria or are not initiated within the specified timeframe.
  • This return policy is subject to change, and any updates will be reflected on our website.

Thank you for shopping with SHOP I.O. We appreciate your understanding and cooperation.
